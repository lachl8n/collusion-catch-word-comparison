How It Works

How does it all work you may ask? How can we test the similarity of text with such key-point precision and accuracy? How can you trust us to provide a faithful comparison of these documents to be the jury of one of the most heinous crimes? It all lies in the source code, and the mathematical calculations! Using a method known as "cosine similarity", we are able to determine the similarity of documents, regardless of their length and size! Derived from the matematical marvel of trigonometry, developed by the Egyptians' early study of triangles in the 2nd millenium BC, the program treats each word in a document as its own vector component! In doing so, we convert all the words into vector components and their frequency becomes the coefficient! Using the angle created from the resulting vectors, we are able to determine if their direction, where identical documents will create identical vectors! Truly fascinating stuff!
